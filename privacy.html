Privacy Policy for PantryToGo

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to PantryToGo. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile applications (Merchant and Customer apps).

2. Data We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you create an account or use our services:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and password.
  • Role-Specific Data: Merchant business details or Customer order history.
  • Device Information: We may collect data about your mobile device, such as the device ID and operating system, to improve app performance.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party service providers to power our app:

  • Google Firebase: Used for user authentication, cloud database storage, and crash reporting.
  • Twilio: Used to send transactional SMS and messaging notifications.

Note: These providers process your data according to their own privacy policies.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Facilitate account creation and secure login.
  • Process and manage orders between Merchants and Customers.
  • Send important service notifications via Twilio.
  • Monitor app performance and fix bugs.

5. Account Deletion (Apple Requirement)

We believe you should have control over your data. You may initiate the deletion of your account and all associated personal data directly within the App Settings menu of the PantryToGo app.

Once requested, your account and identifiable data will be removed from our active databases within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including payload encryption and secure JWT-based authentication, to protect your data during transit and at rest.
